Who We are

At Delving Educational Consultancy, we have a deep passion for higher education. Our primary objective is to assist students and their families in successfully navigating the university admissions processes in the US, UK, and Canada. Our aim is to help every student find the perfect university or college that suits their individual needs. We customize our programs and provide personalized guidance to each student, family, or school, ensuring that they receive the most relevant and beneficial information and advice.

Our dedicated team consists of compassionate individuals who possess exceptional expertise in their respective fields. Over the past two decades, we have effectively steered students towards securing admission to renowned institutions worldwide, including prestigious universities like the Ivy League and Oxbridge.

Our unwavering commitment lies in creating a positive impact on higher education and ensuring the future happiness and success of our students.
